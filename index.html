How It Works

The Neuricle wristband pairs with objects in the environment and sends their data directly to the user's brain. For example, the wristband can scan a deck of cards and transmit its contents to the brain, bypassing visual input.

Direct Brain Connection

Neuricle works by creating a seamless interface between the brain's neural signals and the digital or physical world.

Using advanced neurotechnology, devices like Neuricle transmit sensory data directly to the brain, bypassing traditional sensory pathways like sight or touch. This exchange creates an intuitive and instantaneous interaction, blurring the boundaries between thought and reality.
